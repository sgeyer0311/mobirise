Hobbies & Interests

        One of my major hobbies is swimming. I have been swimming my entire life, but started to compete competitively when I was 14. Some of my early accomplishments include place 4th and 8th at the Ohio High School Championships. My Stevens swimming career began in the fall of 2016 and more details about that can be found under the campus involvement section.  
    
        Another interest of mine is community service. In high school, I went to a Jesuit school that required all students to complete a certain number of service hours. This allowed me to immerse myself in the community of Detriot, Michigan. I went on this service trip the summer leading up to my senior year of high school. This was one of my most memorable experinces because it really opened my eyes to the country we live in and the people living here. One of the most memorable experiences I had was teaching a young girl, 14, named Katara how to swim. We went probably close to two hours learning and the look on her face when she made it accross the pool by herself was something I'll never forget. In this instance I realized how lucky and fortunate I was to learn such as basic thing as swimming growing up.
