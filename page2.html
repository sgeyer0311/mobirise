Stevens Varisty Swim Team

At Stevens, I am also a member of the varsity swim team. I swim the 400 Individual Medley and 500 Freestyle. I am a 3 time Empire 8 conference champion.
As part of the swim team I have taken on the roles of SAAC representative and recruitment liaison. As a SAAC representative I act as a liaison between student-athletes and athletic administration, as well as the campus community. We aim to promote positive sportsmanship and leadership through community service, civic involvement, and academic excellence. A a recruiment liaison for the swim team I was in charge of creating events and activites for potential student athletes when they came to visit for the weekend.

Delta Phi Epsilon

On campus I'm a member of Delta Phi Epsilon sorority and I joined in the fall of 2016. As part a sister of this sorority I have taken a few leadership positions; Untied Greek Council Representative and Panhellenic Delegate. While I was Untied Greek Council Representative I attend weekly meetings with representatives from all other Greek organizations on campus. Our main objective is to plan Greek week which takes place in the spring semester. As a Panhellenic Delegates we strive to improve and work together as greek women on campus. My role is to be the voice of sorority and represent them when voting.
